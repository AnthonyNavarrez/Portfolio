Problem

New Students need help exploring study spots and discovering where to study on/near campus. Without guidance, they rely on word-of-mouth or trial-and-error, wasting time bouncing between locations to find a spot that matches their needs

Returning Students need real-time information about study locations. Even if they know the campus, spots change (renovations, seasonal closures, new spaces open), crowd patterns vary by time/semester, and their study needs evolve.

How Den solves this

A mobile app that visualizes study spots on a map, allowing users to discover:

1.

New Study Locations with their live capacity, rating, and directions. Save your favorite spots!

2.

Apply specific filter to find only study spots that satisfy all your study needs

3.

Read community reviews and leave your own
